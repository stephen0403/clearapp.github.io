Privacy Policy

Effective: June 13, 2026

Thank you for using clear. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, how we use it, and — just as importantly — what we never do with it.

Photo Library Access

clear. requests access to your photo library solely to display photos for you to review. All photo analysis (identifying screenshots, burst shots, duplicates, and old photos) happens entirely on your device using Apple's PhotoKit framework. No photos, thumbnails, or metadata are ever uploaded to any server.

Data We Collect

clear. stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard UserDefaults and on-device file storage:

  • Asset identifiers of photos you have marked for deletion (so pending deletions survive app restarts)
  • Asset identifiers of photos you have chosen to keep (for the 7-day resurface TTL)
  • Estimated file sizes per asset, cached to avoid recomputing them on every launch
  • Lifetime deletion count, freed storage, streak data, and weekly/category stats (aggregate numbers only — no photo content)

None of this data ever leaves your device. We have no servers that receive or store any of it.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not upload photos, thumbnails, or any image data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics
  • We do not track your behaviour across other apps or websites

Payments

clear. offers an optional Pro subscription purchased exclusively through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We never see or store your payment details. All billing, receipt validation, and subscription management is handled end-to-end by Apple. For payment questions, refunds, or disputes, contact Apple Support.

Children's Privacy

clear. is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle your data, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Significant changes will also be noted in the App Store update release notes.
